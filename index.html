Safety Tips

Always share your live location with trusted contacts when traveling alone.

Trust your instincts - if a situation feels unsafe, remove yourself immediately.

Program emergency numbers into your phone for quick access.

When walking alone, stay in well-lit areas and avoid distractions like phones.

Report an Incident

Help keep other women safe by reporting incidents you've witnessed or experienced.

About Reporting

Your reports help us create a safer community by identifying danger zones and patterns of crime against women.

Reporting Guidelines

  • Provide as much detail as possible without compromising your safety
  • For emergencies, always contact the police first
  • False reports are illegal and harm the community
  • You can choose to report anonymously

Confidentiality

We take your privacy seriously. Personal information is protected and anonymous reports are respected.
