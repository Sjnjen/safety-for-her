Daily Safety Tips

⭐ Always share your live location with a trusted contact when traveling alone
⭐ Trust your instincts - if a situation feels unsafe, remove yourself immediately
⭐ Program emergency numbers into your phone's speed dial
⭐ When walking alone, stay in well-lit areas and avoid wearing headphones
⭐ Learn basic self-defense - it builds confidence and awareness

Report an Incident

Help keep other women safe by reporting incidents anonymously

Why Report?

Your report helps create a safer community by:

Impact of Reporting

  • Identifying high-risk areas for police patrolling
  • Alerting other women to potential dangers
  • Building evidence for policy changes
  • Creating accurate crime statistics

Confidentiality Guarantee

Your privacy is protected. All reports are encrypted and personal information is never shared. Anonymous reports are completely untraceable.
