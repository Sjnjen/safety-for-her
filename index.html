Daily Safety Tips

⭐ Always share your live location with a trusted contact when traveling alone
⭐ Trust your instincts - if a situation feels unsafe, remove yourself immediately
⭐ Program emergency numbers into your phone's speed dial
⭐ When walking alone, stay in well-lit areas and avoid wearing headphones
⭐ Learn basic self-defense - it builds confidence and awareness
⭐ Use the "Share Live Location" feature above to keep friends updated
